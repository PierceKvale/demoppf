Vinyl Wraps

Service 02

Paint Protection Film

PPF (Paint Protection Film) is the ultimate invisible shield for your vehicle's paintwork. It absorbs impact from road debris, resists scratches and chemical stains, and features self-healing technology that removes minor swirl marks with heat.

We offer partial front-end kits and full-vehicle coverage, depending on your needs and budget.

Service 03

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic Pro coatings bond at a molecular level to your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and UV damage. Your car stays cleaner longer and retains its gloss for years.

We offer packages from entry-level Bronze to comprehensive Gold packages with full paint and glass coverage.

Window Tinting

Texas heat is no joke. Our ceramic window tints block up to 99% of UV rays, significantly reduce cabin heat, protect your interior from fading, and add a clean, polished look to any vehicle.

Our Story

Passion for cars.
Obsession with quality.

We started as a small operation in Houston and grew into one of the city's most trusted vehicle customization studios — one satisfied customer at a time.

Who We Are

SafeWebsite Demo is a full-service vehicle protection and customization studio based in Dallas, TX. We've been in business for over 8 years and have served more than 2,400 vehicles — from daily commuters to collector-grade exotics.


Our team of 12 certified installers works out of a climate-controlled facility equipped with everything needed to produce showroom-quality results. We don't rush jobs. We don't cut corners. Every vehicle gets treated like it's our own.

Our Mission

We believe your vehicle should look exactly the way you want it to — and stay protected for years to come. That's why we only use manufacturer-backed products, stay certified with industry-leading brands, and invest in ongoing training for every member of our team.


When you bring your car to us, you're not just getting a service — you're getting a dedicated partner who cares about the outcome as much as you do.
